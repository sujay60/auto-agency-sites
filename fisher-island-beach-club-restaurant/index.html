Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require a reservation?

While walk-ins are sometimes accommodated, we highly recommend securing a reservation due to our exclusive island location.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain an "Island Chic" dress code. Resort wear is appropriate during lunch, while elegant casual attire is preferred for evening dining.

Do you offer private event hosting?

Yes, our venue is available for private celebrations and corporate functions. Please contact us to discuss bespoke menu options.
