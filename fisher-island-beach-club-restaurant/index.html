An Island Culinary Sanctuary

Welcome to Fisher Island Beach Club Restaurant, an oasis of sophisticated flavor located at the heart of Florida’s most exclusive enclave. Our mission is to provide an unparalleled dining experience that celebrates the abundance of the Atlantic coast.

Our executive chefs draw inspiration from global Mediterranean influences and local Miami freshness. Every dish is a testament to quality, utilizing locally sourced, sustainable ingredients that ensure our menu remains as vibrant as the island landscape surrounding us.

Whether you are joining us for a sunset cocktail or a refined multi-course celebration, our team is dedicated to perfecting every detail of your visit. We invite you to lose track of time while enjoying our curated wine list and breathtaking waterfront vistas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation to dine here?

Yes, given our exclusive location and high demand, we highly recommend booking your table in advance to ensure your preferred seating.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain an 'Island Chic' dress code, balancing relaxed comfort with upscale elegance. Smart casual attire is encouraged.

Are there gluten-free or vegan options?

Absolutely. Our menu is designed with flexibility in mind, and our chefs are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.
