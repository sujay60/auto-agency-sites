Our Story

Founded on the principles of passion, quality, and hospitality, Chandler's Place has become a staple of the Coral Gables dining scene. We believe that a meal is more than just sustenance; it is a moment to pause, connect, and celebrate the simple joy of exceptional food.

Our culinary team draws inspiration from seasonal ingredients sourced from local Floridian farms, ensuring that every dish plated is a testament to freshness and authenticity. We blend traditional techniques with modern creativity to deliver flavors that resonate with every guest.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone anniversary, closing a business deal, or simply enjoying a weeknight dinner, our refined atmosphere and dedicated service team ensure your experience is nothing short of extraordinary.
