Crafting Moments in Every Cup

At Seaview Cafe, we believe that coffee is more than just a morning necessity; it is a ritual. Nestled in the heart of Palm Beach, our shop brings a refined approach to the traditional cafe experience, sourcing only the finest beans from sustainable, high-altitude farms across the globe.

Our master baristas are dedicated to the science of extraction, ensuring every pour-over, espresso shot, and latte is served with precision. We invite you to step away from the bustle of daily life and find solace in our curated interior, designed specifically for comfort and inspiration.

Whether you are a local regular or a visitor enjoying the coastal charm of Florida, our mission is to provide an unparalleled environment where community, flavor, and elegance meet. Join us at our Seaview Avenue home and discover why we are rated the top destination for coffee lovers in Palm Beach.
