Crafting Mornings Since 2015

At Eli's Cafe, we believe that breakfast is the heartbeat of the day. Located in the vibrant heart of Coral Gables, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing the finest organic ingredients from local Florida farms to create dishes that nourish both body and soul.

Our kitchen is a place of passion where traditional recipes meet modern culinary flair. Whether you're here for a quick espresso on your commute or a leisurely Sunday brunch with family, we treat every plate as a masterpiece.

Beyond the food, Eli's Cafe is a community hub. We pride ourselves on the warm smiles of our staff and the cozy, rustic ambiance that makes every customer feel like a neighbor returning home.
