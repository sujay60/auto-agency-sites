Crafted with Passion & Fresh Ingredients

At Toucan Cafe, we believe that brunch is more than just a meal—it's a lifestyle. Established in the heart of West Palm Beach, our kitchen serves as a sanctuary for those who appreciate the art of breakfast. We source our produce from local Florida farms to ensure every bite is bursting with freshness.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to combine high-end culinary techniques with the cozy, welcoming atmosphere of a local cafe. Whether you're stopping by for a quick morning latte or settling in for a long weekend brunch, our team is dedicated to providing an experience that lingers on your palate long after you leave.

We take pride in our diverse menu, ranging from classic comfort foods with a modern twist to innovative plant-based options that satisfy every dietary preference and craving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

We prioritize walk-ins but highly recommend reservations for weekends, as our brunch service is quite popular!

Are you pet friendly?

Yes! Our outdoor patio is fully pet-friendly, so feel free to bring your furry friends along for breakfast.

Do you offer vegan options?

Absolutely. We offer a wide range of plant-based dishes, including vegan pancakes and gluten-free bread options.
