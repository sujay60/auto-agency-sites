FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?+

Yes! All our memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with no penalties.

Is there a setup or enrollment fee?+

No. We believe in transparent pricing. The price you see is the price you pay, with no hidden fees or enrollment charges.

Can I freeze my membership?+

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year at no additional cost. Just let us know in advance.

Do you offer day passes?+

Yes! Day passes are available for $15. It's a great way to experience Iron Pulse before committing to a membership.
