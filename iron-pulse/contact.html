FAQs

Common Questions

Do I need to book classes in advance?+

Yes, we recommend booking through our app or at the front desk to guarantee your spot, especially for popular classes.

Can I try a class before joining?+

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary trial class for all new visitors. Just book a tour and we'll set it up.

What should I bring to my first visit?+

Just bring comfortable workout clothes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide everything else including lockers.

Do you offer personal training?+

Yes! Personal training is included in our Elite plan, or available as add-on sessions for other membership tiers.
