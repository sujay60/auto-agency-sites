A Legacy of Gastronomy

Theapolis represents more than just a restaurant; it is a sanctuary for the palate, meticulously crafted to reflect the exclusivity of Fisher Island. Our chefs source only the finest local ingredients to create dishes that tell a story of tradition, innovation, and passion.

Whether you are joining us for an intimate dinner or a grand celebration, our team is dedicated to providing an unparalleled service standard that mirrors the beauty of our waterfront surroundings.

We believe that dining is an art form. By blending contemporary culinary techniques with timeless hospitality, we have become a premier destination for discerning gourmands visiting the Miami area.
