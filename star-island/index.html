Frequently Asked Questions

Is Star Island accessible to the public? ▼

Star Island maintains high security to protect the privacy of our residents and guests. Access is restricted to residents and registered visitors only.

How do I reach the island? ▼

The island is connected by a causeway from the MacArthur Causeway in Miami Beach, providing easy vehicular access for residents and guests.

Are there docking facilities? ▼

Yes, many properties on Star Island feature private deep-water docks suitable for large luxury yachts and sport fishing vessels.
