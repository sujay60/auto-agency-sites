Frequently Answered Queries

How do I register as a new family patient at Cumberland?
Registering is simple. Click "Book Now" or fill out the secure intake form on our contact panel. A clinical intake coordinator will schedule a premium initial file review and meet-and-greet with our physicians to map out your long-term electronic health chart.
Are there medical staff with links to Albany Street healthcare clusters?
Yes, our board-certified clinical specialists maintain long-standing collaborative connections with Albany Street family systems and the University of Toronto clinical teaching clusters. This ensures premium diagnostic access and seamless external physician consulting paths.
Do you offer dedicated executive physical assessments?
Absolutely. Our modern boutique medical suite features executive screening tracks designed for rapid clinical diagnostic assessment of biomarkers, high-resolution electrocardiograms, and intensive lifestyle risk profiles.
Is parking available at the Cumberland location?
Yes, secured indoor underground parking is conveniently located underneath Cumberland St. and adjacent municipal commercial garages on Albany Street, just a short, elevator-connected walk to Suite 200.
