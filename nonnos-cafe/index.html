Our Story

At Nonno's Cafe, we believe that coffee is more than just a morning ritual; it is a bridge that connects community and conversation. Nestled in the vibrant neighborhood of Coral Gables, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing only the finest single-origin beans, roasted to perfection to capture the unique nuances of each harvest.

Inspired by the classic European sidewalk cafes, our space was designed to be a sanctuary. Whether you are holding a business meeting, catching up with an old friend, or enjoying a quiet moment with a book, our team ensures every detail reflects our commitment to quality and warmth.

We are proud to serve our community at 550 Biltmore Way, bringing a touch of authentic Italian heritage to Florida. From our golden-crust pastries baked daily to our signature espresso blends, every cup tells a story of passion and persistence.
