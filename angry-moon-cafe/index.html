Our Story & Heritage

Founded on the principles of slow-food and authentic hospitality, Angry Moon Cafe is more than a restaurant; it is a tribute to the diverse culinary traditions of Europe. We believe that great meals are the foundation of great memories, and we pour our passion into every plate we serve.

Our kitchen is driven by a commitment to quality. We source fresh, seasonal ingredients locally whenever possible, pairing them with imported artisanal spices and techniques passed down through generations. Whether you are looking for an intimate dinner for two or a celebratory feast, our atmosphere is crafted to provide comfort and elegance.

Our team of European-trained chefs brings a unique perspective to 2401 PGA Blvd. By combining time-honored techniques with a modern flair, we ensure that every guest experiences the soul of European gastronomy in the heart of Florida.
