Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about referrals, clinical hours, and booking details with Dr. Rauchwerger at the Albany Clinic.

Do I need a physician referral to book an appointment?
Yes. Because Dr. Rauchwerger operates as a specialist within the Ontario healthcare framework (OHIP), a referral from a licensed family physician or general practitioner is highly recommended to secure coverage and schedule diagnostic analysis.
Where is the office located and is there parking?
The office is located within the historic Albany Clinic at 807 Broadview Ave, Toronto, ON. Ample street parking is available along Broadview Ave and nearby side streets. The facility is also highly accessible via public transit, being just steps away from the Broadview Subway Station.
What should I bring to my first consultation?
Please bring a valid Ontario Health Insurance Card (OHIP), a complete list of all active medications (including vitamins and over-the-counter supplements), and copies of any relevant physical lab results or imaging studies from past medical reviews.
How can my physician send referral documents?
Your referring physician can submit all patient packets directly to the central medical administration staff of the Albany Clinic specialists.
