Our Story & Offerings

A Warm Spot in Interlaken

Nestled in the heart of Interlaken, Velo Cafe is more than just a coffee shop – it's a community hub. We believe in good coffee, great company, and a welcoming atmosphere for everyone. Whether you're a local, a traveler, or a cycling enthusiast, you'll find your perfect spot here.

Our journey began with a passion for quality brews and fresh, delicious bites, all served with a smile. We're proud to be a accessible space where everyone can relax and enjoy.

Crafted Delights & More

From meticulously brewed espresso to creamy lattes, our coffee menu is designed to delight every palate. We source premium beans to ensure every cup is a memorable experience.

Complement your drink with our selection of freshly baked pastries, artisanal sandwiches, and light bites. Made with love and local ingredients whenever possible, they're the perfect accompaniment to your Velo Cafe visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
Velo Cafe is open Monday to Friday from 7 AM to 6 PM, and on weekends from 8 AM to 5 PM. We look forward to seeing you!
Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?
Yes, we strive to cater to various dietary needs. We always have a selection of delicious vegan pastries and gluten-free snacks available. Please ask our staff for today's specific offerings.
Can I make a reservation for a table?
While we operate mostly on a walk-in basis, for larger groups (6+ people) or special occasions, we recommend calling us in advance to check availability and potentially reserve a table.
Is Velo Cafe pet-friendly?
Well-behaved dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area. For the comfort of all our guests, we kindly ask that pets remain outside. Water bowls are available upon request!
