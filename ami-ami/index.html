The Ami ami Philosophy

Welcome to Ami ami, a haven for coffee enthusiasts nestled in the heart of Coral Gables. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional coffee heritage and modern, artisanal brewing techniques.

We source only the finest ethically grown beans, ensuring that every sip you take supports sustainable farming communities. Our baristas are meticulously trained to respect the integrity of the bean, bringing out complex notes of chocolate, stone fruit, and earth.

More than just a coffee shop, we strive to create a "third space"—a warm, inviting environment where neighbors become friends and ideas are born over perfectly frothed lattes.

Artisanal Offerings

Single-Origin Brews

Signature Espresso

Our custom espresso blend is the backbone of our shop. Rich, bold, and perfectly balanced, it serves as the base for all our handcrafted milk-based drinks.
