Locate Our Clinic: Your Path to Accessible Healthcare

Albany Medical Clinic is strategically positioned at 807 Broadview Ave, making it a highly accessible destination for residents across Toronto, especially those in the East York, Riverdale, and Danforth communities. We’ve ensured our location is easily reachable by various modes of transport. Use the dynamic map below to pinpoint our exact location and generate personalized directions from your starting point.

Comprehensive Directions to Our Door

  • By TTC (Toronto Public Transit): Our clinic is exceptionally well-served by public transportation. We are conveniently located just a five-minute walk north of Broadview Subway Station, which is on the Bloor-Danforth (Line 2) subway line. Upon exiting the station, simply head north on Broadview Avenue, and you will find our building on your right-hand side. Several key streetcar routes, including the 504 King and 505 Dundas, also have stops within close proximity.
  • By Car: If you are driving, Albany Medical Clinic is easily reachable from major arterial roads. From the Don Valley Parkway (DVP), take the Bloor Street East exit. Proceed west on Bloor Street, then make a right turn onto Broadview Avenue. Continue heading north, and our clinic will be visible on the east side of the street. Please note that while limited street parking may be available, we recommend exploring nearby paid parking lots for extended visits.
  • Accessibility Features: We are deeply committed to ensuring that our clinic is accessible to everyone. Our facility features barrier-free entry points, including ramp access, and elevators are readily available to transport patients to all clinic levels. If you have any specific mobility requirements or require special assistance during your visit, please do not hesitate to inform our reception staff when booking your appointment or upon your arrival.
