Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a new patient at Albany Medical Clinic?

Registering is straightforward. You may utilize our digital booking consultation request form above, or call our Broadview front administration desk at +1 416-461-9471. Our coordinators will arrange your comprehensive intake session immediately.

Where exactly is the clinic situated in Toronto?

Our address is 807 Broadview Ave, Toronto, ON M4K 2P8, Canada. We are nestled conveniently near the Broadview subway station, which provides immediate transit access for patients coming from all corners of Toronto.

Does Albany Medical Clinic accept provincial health insurance (OHIP)?

Yes, standard medical procedures and core clinical examinations are fully covered under OHIP. Premium private wellness panels, customized corporate health packages, or diagnostic elements without provincial backing can be finalized via select private health coverages.

What should I bring to my very first medical appointment?

Please arrive 15 minutes prior to your designated consultation. Bring a valid OHIP health card, any primary pharmacological prescriptions you currently take, and previous diagnostic laboratory reports from other facilities if applicable.
