Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book an appointment?

A: Booking an appointment at Albany Medical Clinic is straightforward and convenient. You can schedule your visit by calling our reception desk at +1 416-461-9471 during business hours, or by utilizing our secure online booking portal available here. For new patients, we recommend reviewing the "New Patient Information" section to ensure you have all necessary documents ready for your first visit. Our team is always ready to assist you in finding a suitable time slot that aligns with your schedule and medical needs.

Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?

A: For your initial visit, please bring your valid health card (OHIP card for Ontario residents), a list of any current medications (including dosages), any relevant medical records or test results you may have, and a form of photo identification. We also encourage you to complete our New Patient Registration Forms in advance, which can be found in the "New Patient Forms" section, to expedite your check-in process and allow more time with your physician.

Q: What are your clinic hours?

A: Albany Medical Clinic operates Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and all statutory holidays. For urgent medical concerns outside of these hours, please visit your nearest emergency department or call 911. You can always confirm specific holiday hours by checking our "Contact Us" page or by giving us a call.

Q: Do you accept new patients?

A: Yes, Albany Medical Clinic is currently accepting new patients! We welcome individuals and families seeking dedicated and compassionate primary care. To register as a new patient, please visit our "New Patient Forms" section to download and complete the necessary paperwork. You can then submit these forms in person or via fax, and our team will contact you to schedule your initial meet-and-greet appointment. We look forward to becoming your trusted healthcare partner.

Transparent Billing Information

At Albany Medical Clinic, we are committed to transparent and clear communication regarding all aspects of your healthcare, including billing. We understand that navigating medical expenses can sometimes be complex, and our goal is to make this process as straightforward as possible for you.

Most services provided at our clinic are covered by the Ontario Health Insurance Plan (OHIP), provided you have a valid OHIP card. However, it's important to be aware that certain services, such as some medical reports, specific immunizations not covered by OHIP, or cosmetic procedures, may incur out-of-pocket costs.

Understanding Our Policies:

  • OHIP Coverage: Please ensure your OHIP card is valid and presented at each visit. Expired cards may result in temporary charges for services, which can be reimbursed upon presentation of a valid card.
  • Uninsured Services: For services not covered by OHIP, we will inform you of the fees in advance. Payment for these services is due at the time of your appointment. We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash.
  • Missed Appointments: To ensure fair access to care for all our patients, a fee may be charged for missed appointments or appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice. Please refer to our Clinic Policies for detailed information.
  • Prescription Renewals & Referrals: In some cases, prescription renewals or specialist referrals may require an in-person or virtual consultation to ensure proper medical oversight. Fees may apply if these are requested outside of a formal consultation.

Should you have any questions or require clarification on our billing procedures or specific service costs, please do not hesitate to speak with our administrative staff. We are here to assist you and ensure a smooth financial experience.

Our Commitment: Albany Medical Clinic Policies

Appointment & Cancellation Policy

To ensure efficient scheduling and maximize access to care for all patients, we kindly request a minimum of 24 hours' notice for any appointment cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the slot to another patient who may be in urgent need. Failure to provide adequate notice or a "no-show" for a scheduled appointment may result in a cancellation fee, which is not covered by OHIP. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in helping us manage our appointments effectively.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your privacy is paramount at Albany Medical Clinic. We adhere strictly to all provincial and federal privacy legislation, including the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA) in Ontario. All your personal health information is handled with the utmost confidentiality and security. Access to your records is limited to authorized personnel directly involved in your care. For a comprehensive understanding of how your information is protected, please review our detailed Privacy Policy available at our reception or downloadable in the "New Patient Forms" section.

Prescription Refill Policy

For your safety and optimal health management, all prescription refills require a proper assessment by your physician. We do not generally refill prescriptions over the phone or without a direct consultation. Please book an appointment well in advance of running out of medication to ensure continuity of your treatment. This policy allows your doctor to monitor your condition, assess medication effectiveness, and make any necessary adjustments to your treatment plan. Urgent requests outside of this process will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and may incur an administrative fee.

Zero Tolerance for Abuse Policy

Albany Medical Clinic is dedicated to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all patients and staff. We uphold a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of verbal, emotional, or physical abuse, harassment, discrimination, or aggressive behavior. Such conduct will not be tolerated and may result in the termination of the patient-clinic relationship and, if necessary, involvement of law enforcement. We believe in mutual respect and clear communication as the foundation for effective healthcare.

For a complete list of all clinic policies, please ask our reception staff.
