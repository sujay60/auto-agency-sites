Our Esteemed Physicians

Dr. Anya Sharma

Specialist in Internal Medicine & Chronic Disease Management

Dr. Anya Sharma brings over 15 years of dedicated experience to Albany Medical Clinic, specializing in Internal Medicine with a profound focus on the comprehensive management of chronic diseases. A graduate of the University of Toronto, Dr. Sharma completed her residency at St. Michael's Hospital, where she honed her expertise in complex diagnostic challenges and long-term patient care strategies. She is renowned for her meticulous approach to patient health, believing strongly in empowering individuals through education and shared decision-making.

Her philosophy centers on a holistic view of health, considering not just physical symptoms but also the lifestyle, emotional, and social factors that influence well-being. Dr. Sharma is particularly skilled in developing personalized treatment plans for conditions suchs as diabetes, hypertension, and cardiovascular diseases, emphasizing preventative measures and sustained health improvements. Patients appreciate her empathetic demeanor, thorough explanations, and unwavering commitment to their journey towards optimal health. Her dedication extends beyond the clinic, actively participating in public health initiatives to promote wellness in the Toronto community.

Dr. Sharma's commitment to continuous learning keeps her at the forefront of medical advancements, ensuring that her patients receive the most current and effective care. She is a firm advocate for integrative medicine, understanding the intricate connections within the human body and developing strategies that address the root causes of illness. Her clinic hours are structured to allow ample time for patient consultations, fostering an environment where every concern is heard and addressed with the utmost attention.

Dr. Julian Vance

Family Medicine & Geriatric Care

Dr. Julian Vance is a cornerstone of Albany Medical Clinic's Family Medicine department, bringing over two decades of invaluable experience in providing comprehensive care across all age groups, with a particular passion for geriatric care. A distinguished alumnus of McGill University's Faculty of Medicine, Dr. Vance completed his residency in Family Medicine at Mount Sinai Hospital in Toronto. His commitment to building lasting patient relationships and offering continuous care across generations is a hallmark of his practice.

His patient care philosophy is rooted in empathy, clear communication, and preventative strategies. Dr. Vance believes in fostering a collaborative environment where patients feel heard and are active participants in their health decisions. He specializes in routine check-ups, acute illness management, and the complex needs of older adults, including polypharmacy management and cognitive health. His gentle approach and extensive knowledge make him a trusted advisor for families seeking long-term medical support.

Beyond his clinical duties, Dr. Vance is actively involved in community health education, frequently giving talks on healthy aging and disease prevention. He is dedicated to creating a welcoming and supportive environment for all patients, ensuring that everyone receives the personalized attention and respect they deserve. His tireless dedication to improving the quality of life for his patients, particularly the elderly, underscores his profound impact on the Albany Medical Clinic community.

Dr. Isabella Rossi

Pediatrics & Adolescent Health

Dr. Isabella Rossi is the beloved pediatrician at Albany Medical Clinic, known for her vibrant energy and profound understanding of children's health. With a medical degree from Queen's University and a pediatric residency completed at The Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids), Dr. Rossi is a highly sought-after expert in infant, child, and adolescent care. Her clinic is a warm and inviting space where young patients feel comfortable and parents feel supported.

Her philosophy emphasizes preventive care, early intervention, and open communication with both children and their guardians. Dr. Rossi expertly navigates the unique challenges of pediatric medicine, from routine vaccinations and developmental milestones to managing complex childhood conditions. She believes in nurturing a positive relationship with health from an early age, equipping families with the knowledge and tools to promote lifelong wellness for their children.

Dr. Rossi's patient care extends to advocating for children's health within the broader community, collaborating with schools and local organizations to promote healthy lifestyles and access to care. Her ability to connect with children on their level, coupled with her rigorous medical training, makes her an invaluable asset to our team and a comforting presence for countless families. She is passionate about creating a foundation of health that helps every child thrive.

Dr. Michael Lee

General Practice & Minor Procedures

Dr. Michael Lee is a highly respected General Practitioner at Albany Medical Clinic, known for his approachable nature and comprehensive medical expertise. Having graduated from McMaster University and completed his family medicine residency in Toronto, Dr. Lee has a strong background in managing a wide array of health concerns for adults and adolescents. He is also proficient in performing various minor in-office procedures, offering convenience and efficient care to his patients.

His patient care philosophy emphasizes accessibility, clear communication, and pragmatic solutions. Dr. Lee believes in building a trusting relationship with each patient, ensuring they feel comfortable discussing their health concerns openly. He focuses on timely diagnoses, effective treatment plans, and thorough follow-up, ensuring continuity of care. Patients particularly value his ability to explain complex medical information in an understandable manner.

Dr. Lee's dedication to patient comfort extends to his skill in minor surgical procedures, including skin lesion removals, suturing, and joint injections, performed with precision and care. He continually updates his medical knowledge, embracing new techniques and guidelines to provide the highest standard of care. His commitment to the well-being of his patients makes him a vital member of the Albany Medical Clinic team, ensuring that quality healthcare is always within reach.

Dr. Sophia Chang

Women's Health & Preventative Medicine

Dr. Sophia Chang is a compassionate and dedicated physician focusing on Women's Health and preventative medicine at Albany Medical Clinic. With a medical degree from the University of Western Ontario and specialized training in women's health issues during her residency, Dr. Chang offers expert care across all stages of a woman's life. She is passionate about empowering women to take charge of their health through education and proactive wellness strategies.

Her approach to patient care is deeply empathetic and personalized. Dr. Chang understands the unique health challenges women face and provides comprehensive services ranging from routine gynecological exams, contraception counseling, and prenatal care to menopausal management and bone health. She fosters an environment of trust and open dialogue, ensuring every patient feels comfortable discussing sensitive health topics.

Dr. Chang is a staunch advocate for preventative health, guiding her patients through screenings, lifestyle adjustments, and nutritional advice to minimize health risks. She is committed to staying abreast of the latest research and advancements in women's health, continuously integrating evidence-based practices into her care. Her gentle nature and profound expertise make her an invaluable resource for women seeking comprehensive and supportive healthcare.

Dr. Ethan Miller

Sports Medicine & Rehabilitation

Dr. Ethan Miller is the go-to expert for Sports Medicine and rehabilitation at Albany Medical Clinic, serving athletes and active individuals of all ages. A graduate of the University of Toronto with a fellowship in Sports Medicine, Dr. Miller brings a deep understanding of musculoskeletal injuries, exercise physiology, and recovery strategies. He is passionate about helping patients return to their peak performance safely and efficiently.

His patient care philosophy is centered on functional recovery and injury prevention. Dr. Miller employs a holistic approach, combining accurate diagnosis with personalized treatment plans that often include targeted exercises, injections, and referrals to physical therapy. He works closely with patients to understand their athletic goals and lifestyle, tailoring interventions to ensure the best possible outcomes and long-term joint health.

Dr. Miller's extensive experience includes working with various sports teams and active individuals, from amateur enthusiasts to professional athletes. He stays current with the latest advancements in sports medicine, incorporating innovative techniques to accelerate recovery and prevent recurrence of injuries. His dedication to restoring mobility, reducing pain, and optimizing physical function makes him an invaluable resource for anyone seeking to maintain an active, healthy lifestyle.
