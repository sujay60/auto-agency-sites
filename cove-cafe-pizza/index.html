A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Nestled on the beautiful shores of West Palm Beach, Cove Cafe & Pizza was born from a simple mission: to bring authentic, stone-fired pizza and locally sourced coffee to our community. We believe that great food is the cornerstone of great memories.

Our chefs utilize time-honored techniques, hand-stretching dough daily and sourcing only the finest mozzarella and freshest garden vegetables. Whether you are grabbing a quick morning espresso or settling in for a family dinner, we provide an atmosphere of genuine hospitality.

From the first bite of our signature crust to the last sip of our specialty lattes, every item is crafted with intention. We are proud to be a staple in the Flagler Drive neighborhood, where every guest is treated like family.

Our Culinary Offerings

Artisanal Pizza

Hand-tossed dough, fermented for 48 hours, topped with premium artisanal ingredients and fire-baked in our custom oven.

Gourmet Cafe

Sustainably sourced coffee beans roasted to perfection, paired with locally baked pastries and refreshing seasonal refreshments.

Event Catering

Bring the taste of The Cove to your special events, birthdays, or corporate lunches with our customized catering menus.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We prioritize inclusivity by offering high-quality gluten-free cauliflower crusts and a selection of plant-based cheeses and vegetables.

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are always welcome, we highly recommend booking a table for weekend evenings to ensure you don't miss out on your favorite spot.

Where are you located?

We are conveniently located at 4340 N Flagler Dr, West Palm Beach, FL 33407, right along the scenic waterfront.
