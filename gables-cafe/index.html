Located in the heart of Coral Gables, Gables Cafe brings generations of Cuban culinary expertise to your table. From our slow-roasted Lechon Asado to our velvety flan, every dish is prepared with passion and the finest ingredients.

Common Questions

Do you offer vegan or vegetarian options?

Yes! We offer a variety of authentic Cuban side dishes, including yuca with mojo, moros y cristianos, and fresh seasonal salads.

Is there parking available near the cafe?

Street parking is available on Almeria Ave, and there is a public parking garage within two blocks of our location.

Absolutely. Please contact us via phone or email to discuss catering or private dining arrangements for your special occasions.
