A Tradition of Flavor

Gables Cafe was born from a passion for authentic Cuban heritage. Located in the heart of Coral Gables, we bring the warmth and zest of island culture to every plate we serve. Our family recipes have been passed down for generations, ensuring that every bite tells a story of tradition.

We believe that great food starts with fresh, quality ingredients. From our daily-baked Cuban bread to our signature slow-marinated pork, we source only the finest produce and meats to recreate the nostalgic tastes of Old Havana right here in Florida.

Whether you are visiting us for a morning café con leche or a hearty evening feast, our team is dedicated to providing an atmosphere that feels like home. Join us and discover why our community considers us a local gem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! We feature several vegetarian-friendly Cuban dishes, including our famous yucca fries, plantains, and black bean bowls.

We offer a beautiful, shaded outdoor patio area, perfect for enjoying the Coral Gables weather while you dine.
