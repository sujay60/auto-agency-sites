Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in Miami do you specialize in?

We specialize in Miami's most exclusive neighborhoods, including Brickell, South Beach, Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, Key Biscayne, and Sunny Isles Beach. Our deep local knowledge ensures we can find the perfect property for your needs across these coveted areas.

How do you help clients find off-market properties?

Our extensive network and strong relationships within the luxury market often give us access to exclusive off-market listings. We proactively connect with owners and other agents to uncover hidden gems that aren't publicly advertised, giving our clients a distinct advantage.

What is your process for selling a luxury property?

Our selling process begins with a comprehensive market analysis and property valuation. We then develop a tailored, high-impact marketing strategy, including professional photography, videography, targeted digital campaigns, and exclusive showings to qualified buyers. We handle all negotiations to secure the best possible outcome for you.

Do you assist international clients with relocation?

Absolutely. Miami is a global hub, and we regularly assist international clients with their relocation needs. This includes not only finding the perfect property but also connecting them with trusted resources for legal, financial, and logistical aspects of moving to Miami.
