Frequently Asked Questions

Find swift answers regarding enrollment, walk-in triages, and building accessibility.

How do I enroll in Albany's Family Practice network?
We are currently registering new family medicine patients. Simply call our dedicated booking desk at +1 416-366-2000 or complete our digital medical questionnaire in our secure intake portal. Once submitted, our triage clerk will arrange a brief initial screening with our onboarding physicians.
What is the average wait time for the Walk-In Clinic?
Our walk-in medical clinic is structured with optimized triage processing, meaning urgent trauma cases are stabilized immediately while standard acute cases maintain an average wait time under 15-20 minutes. It operates six days a week for medical convenience.
Do you provide diagnostic lab testing directly inside the building?
Yes. Albany Medical Building features integrated fully-equipped medical laboratory and diagnostic facilities. Rapid bloodwork draws, imaging pathways, urgent testing sequences, and cardiovascular diagnostics are executed in-house for seamless medical verification.
Is there ample vehicle parking and transit access available?
Yes, we have full accessibility parameters in place. There is designated patient vehicle parking behind the clinic building with elevator access to all medical floors. Additionally, we are conveniently situated right on Broadview Ave, just a minute's walk north of the Broadview subway station.
How are clinical prescriptions routed directly?
During your appointment consultation, your physician can transmit prescriptions directly via secure E-routing instantly to the on-site clinic partner pharmacy, or any pharmacy of your preference across Ontario, minimizing your wait time significantly.
