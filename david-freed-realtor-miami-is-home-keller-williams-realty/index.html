Frequently Asked Questions

How much is my home worth in the current Miami market?
Determining your home's accurate value requires a comprehensive market analysis. We'll assess comparable sales, current market trends, property condition, and unique features to provide you with a precise valuation. Contact us for a complimentary, no-obligation home valuation report.
What is the process for buying a home in Miami?
The home buying process typically involves pre-approval for a mortgage, identifying your needs and preferences, searching for properties, making an offer, negotiating, conducting inspections and appraisals, and finally, closing. David Freed will guide you through each step, ensuring a smooth and informed journey.
Why should I choose David Freed and Keller Williams Realty?
David Freed offers deep local expertise, a client-centric approach, and proven negotiation skills, all backed by the global resources and innovative technology of Keller Williams Realty. We prioritize your goals, provide transparent communication, and strive to make your real estate experience both successful and enjoyable.
What are the current trends in the Miami luxury real estate market?
Miami's luxury market is highly dynamic, often driven by international buyers, new developments, and a strong demand for waterfront properties and high-end amenities. We closely monitor these trends to provide our clients with up-to-the-minute insights and strategic advice.
