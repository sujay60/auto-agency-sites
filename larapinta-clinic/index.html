Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?
While we prefer appointments to minimize your wait time, we do accept walk-ins for urgent non-life-threatening medical issues.
How long is a typical consultation?
A standard consultation is 15-20 minutes. If you have multiple health concerns, please book a long consultation when making your appointment.
Are your facilities fully accessible?
Yes, Larapinta Clinic is completely wheelchair accessible, including all consultation rooms and restrooms.
