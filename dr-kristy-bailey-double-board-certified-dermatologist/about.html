Meet Dr. Kristy Bailey

As a double-board certified dermatologist practicing in the heart of Toronto, Dr. Bailey blends clinical precision with a refined aesthetic sensibility. Her mission is to transform skin health through evidence-based medicine and personalized, compassionate care.

Medical Philosophy

Every patient receives a comprehensive assessment focusing on long-term wellness rather than temporary fixes.
