A Culinary Landmark on Ocean Drive

Founded on the principle that dining is a celebration, Icon Beach Cafe has become a staple of the Miami Beach culinary scene. We believe in high-quality, locally-sourced ingredients paired with an atmosphere that breathes relaxation.

Our kitchen team is dedicated to crafting dishes that tell a story of the coast—from freshly caught seafood to sun-ripened tropical produce. Every guest who walks through our doors is treated to a standard of service that reflects our 4.8-star reputation.

Whether you are visiting for a sunrise breakfast or a candlelit dinner under the stars, we invite you to experience the authentic magic of Miami Beach at Icon Beach Cafe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept reservations for large groups?

Yes, we specialize in hosting intimate gatherings and large parties. Please contact us 48 hours in advance for groups larger than 8.

Are you pet friendly?

Our outdoor terrace is perfectly pet-friendly! Bring your furry companions to enjoy the Miami weather with you.

Is there valet parking nearby?

Yes, there is public parking on Ocean Drive, and several private valet services operate within one block of the entrance.
