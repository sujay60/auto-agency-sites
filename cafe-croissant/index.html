Our Culinary Heritage

At Cafe Croissant, we believe that food is an art form. Inspired by the bustling boulangeries of Paris, we bring the heart of French tradition to the streets of Miami. Every morning, our chefs start before dawn to ensure the freshest experience for our guests.

Our journey began with a simple desire: to share the joy of a perfectly laminated croissant and a rich, aromatic espresso. We source only the finest local ingredients paired with imported French butter and flour to maintain an uncompromising standard of quality.

Whether you're looking for a quick morning pick-me-up or a leisurely weekend brunch, we invite you to sit back and savor the authentic spirit of French hospitality right here in Coral Way.
