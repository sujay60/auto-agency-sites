Crafted with Tradition

At Cafe Croissant, we believe in the art of the perfect breakfast. Our kitchen uses only the finest imported French flour and butter to bring you the authentic taste of Parisian mornings. Whether it's a quick espresso or a leisurely brunch, we invite you to slow down and savor the moment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegan options? +

Yes, we provide several vegan-friendly pastries and plant-based milk alternatives for our coffee beverages.

Do I need a reservation? +

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend reservations on weekends to ensure you get your favorite spot.

Is the outdoor seating pet-friendly? +

Absolutely! Our patio is fully pet-friendly, so feel free to bring your furry friends along.
