Crafting Moments of Sweetness Since Our Inception

JuanCafe was born out of a passion for authentic baking and the joy of sharing high-quality ingredients with our community. Located in the heart of Coral Gables, we take pride in our traditional methods combined with a modern creative flair.

Our mission is simple: to provide every guest with a sensory experience that goes beyond the ordinary. We believe that every celebration, small or grand, deserves a cake that is as delicious as it is beautiful.

Every morning, our kitchen awakens with the scent of golden croissants and freshly prepared dough. We invite you to visit our shop and taste the dedication that goes into every single item we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a rotating selection of gluten-free treats, though we recommend calling ahead for specific availability.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For standard custom cakes, we ask for a minimum of 48 hours notice. For complex designs, please inquire at least one week ahead.

Do you have delivery options?

Yes, we provide local delivery within the Coral Gables area for larger orders. Contact us for delivery rates.
